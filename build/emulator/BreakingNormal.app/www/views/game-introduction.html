GUIDELINES

This is a game of quick answers to inspire authentic responses that make your palms sweat. One person goes at a time moving in a counter-clockwise direction. On your turn press the shuffle button and you will have 30 seconds to answer. The timer is your friend here. You may think 30 seconds is way too short but we've found that this creative constraint is the most powerful way to get someone to bypass the head and speak straight from the heart. You must use the full 30 secs to express your answer, even if it's means just standing there saying "I don't know".

You'll notice a prompt to either "speak it", "sing it", "embody it" or "question master" with each question

SPEAK IT means you speak your answer, SING IT means you sing your answer, QUESTION MASTER means that you create an awesome question for someone else to answer, EMBODY IT means to embody your answer response without words... similar to charades.

The rules are simple;
Be HONEST - Answer with your most vulnerable truth - this game is about getting FUNcomfortable, so really go for it! Express yourself! But remember you only have 30 seconds, so use your time wisely - stop immediately when the time is up. To get the most out of this game, we suggest you avoid giving any feedback or commentary between turns until after the game has been completed to keep the momentum and energy flowing!

TWO IMPORTANT AGREEMENTS

There are two agreements all players in the game can make to create safe space for self-expression through confidentiality. Have everyone raise their hand to show their compliance in creating safe space before you begin.

  • I agree not to share personal information that comes up during the game without consent.
  • I agree to be radically honest and share authentically.
